# Privacy Policy

**Cold Signal**
Developed by O2cool168 Studios

Last updated: July 13, 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how O2cool168 Studios ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Cold Signal (the "Game"). We built Cold Signal to work as a single-player experience, and we designed it to collect as little personal information as possible.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
Cold Signal does not require account creation, sign-in, or the submission of personal information (such as your name, address, or phone number) to play.

### Automatically Collected Information
Depending on the platform and version installed, the Game may collect limited technical information, including:

- Device type, operating system, and version
- App version and settings
- Crash reports and diagnostic logs
- General gameplay analytics (e.g., session length, level progress, feature usage)
- Advertising identifiers (only in the free, ad-supported version, if applicable)

This information is used to fix bugs, improve performance, and understand how players interact with the Game. It is not used to identify you personally.

### In-App Purchases
The free version of Cold Signal may offer optional in-app purchases (cosmetic clothing packs, shelter decoration packs, character skins). Purchases are processed by the app store platform (Apple App Store or Google Play Store), and we do not receive or store your payment card information.

## Third-Party Services

Cold Signal may use third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, and advertising (in the free version only). These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies, including:

- Analytics and crash reporting providers
- Advertising networks (free version only)
- App store platforms (Apple, Google) for purchases and distribution

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## No Pay-to-Win / No Sale of Data

Cold Signal does not include pay-to-win mechanics, and we do not sell player data to third parties for monetary consideration.

## Children's Privacy

Cold Signal is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details below so we can remove it.

## Data Retention

We retain automatically collected technical and analytics data only as long as necessary to support the purposes described in this policy, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

## Your Choices

- You can disable or limit ad tracking through your device's operating system settings.
- You can uninstall the Game at any time, which stops any further local data collection.
- You may contact us to ask questions about the data we process.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Game after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
