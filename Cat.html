Popular Cat Breeds

Maine Coon

The gentle giant. Huge, fluffy, and incredibly friendly. Often called "the dog of the cat world."

Siamese

Chatty, intelligent, and elegant. They love talking to their humans and have striking blue eyes.

Ragdoll

Super relaxed and affectionate. They go limp like a ragdoll when picked up — hence the name!

Persian

The fluffy royalty. Known for their sweet faces, luxurious coats, and calm personalities.

Amazing Cat Facts 🐾

🐾
Cats walk like camels and giraffes — they move both right feet first, then both left feet. No other animal walks this way!
🐾
House cats share 95.6% of their DNA with tigers. They're basically tiny tigers living in your house.
🐾
Cats can't taste sweetness, but they have an extra organ that lets them "taste" smells.
🐾
A group of cats is called a "clowder". A group of kittens is a "kindle".
🐾
Cats can jump up to 6 times their body length and run up to 30 mph in short bursts.
